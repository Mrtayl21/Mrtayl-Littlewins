<h2>Classroom Tips</h2>

<p>

These simple classroom tips can help new teachers feel more confident and successful every day.

</p>


<h3>Tip 1: Start with routines</h3>

<p>

Teaching routines early helps students know what to expect and prevents behavior problems.

</p>



<h3>Tip 2: Be consistent</h3>

<p>

Consistency builds trust and structure in your classroom.

</p>



<h3>Tip 3: Celebrate small wins</h3>

<p>

Even small improvements are important. Recognizing progress builds student confidence and teacher confidence.

</p>



<h3>Tip 4: Stay organized</h3>

<p>

Keeping lesson plans and materials organized saves time and reduces stress.

</p>
