About Little Wins Teaching

Little Wins Teaching is a supportive online space for first-year and early-career elementary teachers.

Our Mission

The mission is simple: provide free resources, practical classroom tips, and encouragement that helps new teachers build confidence one small win at a time.

Who It’s For

This site is designed for teachers who are still learning routines, behavior strategies, lesson planning, and work-life balance. Many new teachers need support, but mentoring is not always consistent in schools—this website helps fill that gap.

Similar Websites

  • Edutopia – research-based strategies and classroom inspiration
